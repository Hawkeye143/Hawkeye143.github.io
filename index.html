Team members

Heena Jeelani

Attorney

Heena received her Bachelors of Arts (B.A.) in Business and Economics from Benedictine University in 2005. Thereafter, she received her Masters of Business Administration (M.B.A.) concentrated in Accounting and International Business as well as an Masters of Science Accounting (M.S.A.) concentrated in Forensic Accounting also from Benedictine University in 2010. She then went on to earn her Juris Doctorate (J.D.) from Western Michigan University Thomas M. Cooley Law School in 2013. While in law school, Heena was a member of the Phi Delta Phi International Legal Honor Society. The Law Office of Heena Jeelani is dedicated to providing quality legal services.
